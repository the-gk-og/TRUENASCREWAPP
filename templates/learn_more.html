Is my data secure?

Yes! All data is stored securely with encryption. Regular backups ensure you never lose important information. You control all access with user accounts and admin privileges.

Can I use this on my phone?

Absolutely! The system is fully responsive and works perfectly on phones, tablets, and desktops. You can even install it as an app on your home screen.

How do I import existing equipment?

Use CSV upload or connect your Google Sheet via SheetDB. No technical knowledge required‚Äîjust a spreadsheet with equipment information.

What if I lose my password?

Contact your administrator. They can reset your password from the admin panel.

Can I use this offline?

The system requires an internet connection to function. However, you can install it as a PWA (Progressive Web App) for faster loading and offline awareness.

How many users can I have?

Unlimited users! Each team member gets their own account with customizable permissions.
